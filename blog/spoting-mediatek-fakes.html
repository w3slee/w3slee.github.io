The mediatek fakes

Apparently the reason why you'll see very many people owning 'high end' flagship devices from the third world Kenya is buying fake devices. The common and popular brands Infinix and Tecno have flooded the Kenyan market making the counterfeiters hard at work, pumping in the fakes. It's not hard to spot a fake by an average consumer who hopefully uses original devices. The give aways usually start on the screen. Devices claiming to have UHD / FHD displays ending up looking so 'plasticky'. And when the light from the sun hits the display it almost looks like a calculators LCD. Ok let's begin with the juicy technicals, this week I got two devices. An infinix X657 and a Tecno F2.

The infinix device had the following specifications 32GB internal memory with 2 Gigs of RAM. Running on android version 9

Running adb shell getprop ro.buid.version.release gives us the correct device properties

The Tecno device had the following specifications 8GB internal memory with 1 Gigs of RAM. Running on android version 6. The original runs android 7.0 (Nougat)
Internal storage properties

These kind of devices are all over the market and very hard to detect it's flaws from unexperienced consumers. Stay educated !
