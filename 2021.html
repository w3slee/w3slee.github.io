socials in the terminal, best of RSS with newsboat

[🏡—read]

Spending too much time 'surfing' and you are capable of using linux ? Well waste no more time. If you are a sucker for minimal software and text based user interfaces turn you on then newsboar is for you. A simple terminal application that updates and relays your subscibed RSS feeds in the terminal. Real blogs and articles straight from the command line. The setup is also childs play as you only need to append the rss feed url in the ~/url config file located in ~/,config/newsboat/url or ~/.newsboat/url depending on where newsboat is installed.
